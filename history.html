The Story of The Sofia

A Legacy Reimagined

This historic building, once owned by the Flocchini Family and Gladding McBean Clay Company, has undergone a transformation.

This thoughtful restoration has focused on preserving its storied past while considering the best use for this property moving forward, paying special attention to modern touches and conveniences. This custom craftsman, built in the 1800s, has entertained many guests over the years.

We present a walk back in time to the charm of a bygone era, coupled with a desire to create a space for families and friends to gather and celebrate a family wedding or gathering, a business meeting, or a friends' get-together. Please come and enjoy this beautifully inspired creation we call The Sofia, the name of which comes from the youngest daughter of the current owner.
