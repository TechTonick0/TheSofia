The Sofia Business Plan presentation
The Sofia desires to operate an event venue to include weddings, business meetings, fundraisers, and family gatherings.  Music will likely accompany wedding events and family gatherings including live music.
Alcohol service will only occur during scheduled events and the venue will not operate as a bar or restaurant.  No alcohol will be stored or sold as part of any regular business operations.  As catering will likely be a regular occurrence on site, we will require that any caterer be licensed with Alcoholic Beverage Control and maintain a Type 58 permit.  Our business is not required to have a permit for alcohol since this will not be part of our business operations.  Caterers would use the ABC 218 application to get the Type 58 permit.
The outdoor space of The Sofia plans to operate from 9 am to Midnight Monday through Saturday.  
We also plan to offer the residence located on the property as a short-term rental, which would allow for out-of-town guests to have a place to stay for the duration of the event.  Also, with more out-of-towners coming to Lincoln for the wine trail and other downtown events, The Sofia would provide a lodging option not necessarily associated with outdoor events.  We would plan to be open for guests 7 days a week with a check out time of noon and a check in time of 3 pm to allow for cleaning and prep for next guests.  An elevator is installed and monitored by the Department of Industrial Relations for guests need to get from the ground floor to the residence.
